# Maps bundled with the project

This package is self-contained. No map downloader is required.

## Difficulty ladder

| Tier | Map | Purpose | Scenarios |
|---|---|---|---|
| very small | `empty-8-8` | correctness and tie sensitivity on an open grid | 3 generated distance tiers, 12 tasks each |
| targeted | `diagnostic/crossing-9-9` | shared intersection and vertex conflicts | 3 hand-designed scenarios |
| targeted | `diagnostic/corridor-passing-11-7` | waiting and passing in a narrow corridor | 3 hand-designed scenarios |
| targeted | `diagnostic/bottleneck-13-9` | ordering through a one-cell doorway | 3 hand-designed scenarios |
| medium | `maze-32-32-2` | long narrow routes and low connectivity | 3 generated distance tiers, 12 tasks each |
| medium | `random-32-32-10` | irregular sparse obstacles | 3 generated distance tiers, 12 tasks each |
| medium-large | `warehouse-10-20-10-2-1` | structured aisles and repeated bottlenecks | 3 generated distance tiers, 12 tasks each |
| large | `room-64-64-16` | the original room benchmark used in development | 3 MovingAI even scenarios |
| large | `warehouse-20-40-10-2-1` | the original large warehouse benchmark | 3 MovingAI even scenarios |
| large | `maze-128-128-10` | the original long-path maze benchmark | 3 MovingAI even scenarios |

The previous three large maps remain unchanged in purpose and are not replaced by the smaller maps.

## Scenario construction for the bundled small/medium maps

For `empty-8-8`, `maze-32-32-2`, `random-32-32-10`, and
`warehouse-10-20-10-2-1`, the geometry is benchmark geometry and the scenario
files were generated specifically for this project. A fixed seed was used for
reproducibility. Candidate start-goal pairs were grouped by sampled four-way
shortest-path distance quantiles:

- scenario 1: lower-distance tier;
- scenario 2: middle-distance tier;
- scenario 3: upper-distance tier.

Each scenario contains 12 tasks whose starts and goals are pairwise disjoint.
This supports task offsets `0`, `4`, and `8` for up to four agents.

The exact distance ranges are recorded in `BUNDLED_MAPS_MANIFEST.json`.

## Provenance

- The large room and warehouse files were extracted from the MovingAI archives supplied with the project.
- The large maze map was extracted from the supplied MovingAI map archive. Its first three even scenario files were obtained from a public benchmark mirror and normalized by adding the `version 1` header required by this loader.
- `empty-8-8` and `warehouse-10-20-10-2-1` geometry came from the public `smart-mapf/smart` mirror.
- `maze-32-32-2` geometry came from the public `MShepelin/MultiAgentPathFinding` mirror.
- `random-32-32-10` geometry came from the public `Kei18/lacam` mirror.
- The three diagnostic maps and their scenarios were generated by this project.
